WHO WE ARE:

We support innovative companies with funding, strategy, and expertise to drive growth and solve critical global challenges.

OxiBio Ventures is a multi-family office specializing in the biotech, pharmaceutical, and IT sectors. Being established on the strong foundation of OXIMIO™, we draw on decades of expertise in clinical trial logistics and global healthcare innovations. Our commitment is to catalyze the development of transformative technologies and treatments that improve human health and technological advancement.

At OxiBio Ventures, we don't just invest in businesses — we build lasting partnerships that push boundaries in science and technology while bringing innovation to people. Our deep industry knowledge and global network position us as a vital partner for founders and companies looking to make a meaningful impact in the biotech, pharmaceutical and IT sectors.
