WHO WE ARE:

We empower innovative companies with funding, strategic insight, and expertise to drive growth and tackle critical global challenges.

OxiBio Ventures is a multi-family office specializing in the biotech, pharmaceutical, and IT sectors. Founded on the solid foundation of OXIMIO™, we bring decades of expertise in clinical trial logistics and global healthcare innovations. Our mission is to accelerate the development of transformative technologies and treatments that enhance human health and technological progress.

At OxiBio Ventures, we do more than invest — we forge lasting partnerships that push the boundaries of science and technology, delivering real-world innovation. With deep industry expertise and a global network, we are the go-to partner for founders and companies looking to make a meaningful impact in biotech, pharmaceuticals, and IT.
